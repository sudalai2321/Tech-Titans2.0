About the Event

Techura is the upcoming Technical Symposium 2K25 organized by the Department of Information Technology, Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University. This vibrant platform brings together young innovators, creators, and tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills, share ideas, and celebrate the power of technology.

The symposium is designed to inspire innovation, encourage collaboration, and build future-ready talent through a wide range of events, including technical paper presentations, coding contests, project expos, workshops, and fun spot events

At Techura, we believe in creating opportunities for students to explore the latest advancements in IT, challenge themselves in competitive events, and network with like-minded peers from diverse institutions

Join us as we Innovate, Compete, and Celebrate Technology – because the future begins here, at Techura

Date: 10th September 2025
